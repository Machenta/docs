## What is K12 Safety?

K12 Safety is a suite of AI agents and workflows designed specifically for K-12 school safety planning. The platform helps you:

- **Generate compliant EOP annexes** — AI-assisted drafting of emergency response procedures
- **Review existing plans** — Automated analysis for gaps, compliance issues, and improvements
- **Create Quick Reference Guides** — Condensed, actionable summaries for emergency responders
- **Ensure consistency** — Standardized formatting and language across all documents

<Info>
All AI-generated content is reviewed for safety and appropriateness before being returned. The platform includes built-in content moderation and compliance checks.
</Info>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Provide the source document or describe the content you need. The platform accepts existing EOP documents, templates, or new content requests.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI processing">
    The K12 Safety agent analyzes your input, applies domain-specific knowledge, and generates draft content following best practices for school safety planning.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and refine">
    Review the generated content, request modifications if needed, and approve the final version. All changes are tracked for audit purposes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export and use">
    Download completed documents in your preferred format. Content is ready for integration into your school's emergency planning materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>
